Controls:
The workstation is directly in front of you.

VR:
- Teleport: Aim with your Left Hand, pull Trigger to move.
- Grab: Reach with your Right Hand and use Trigger/Grip.
- Info: Descriptive text appears above objects when you grab them.

Desktop: WASD to move, Mouse to look.

Goal: Assemble the PC! Dropped parts will respawn if they fall.
