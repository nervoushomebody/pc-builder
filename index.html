Controls:
The PC Case is in front. Components are in a circle around you.

VR:
- Grab: Reach out and use Trigger/Grip buttons.
- Move: Use the Left Thumbstick.
- Turn: Use the Right Thumbstick.
- Info: When you grab an item, a description appears at its side.

Desktop: WASD to move, Mouse to look.

Goal: Pick up components and place them in the PC case! Dropped parts will respawn.
